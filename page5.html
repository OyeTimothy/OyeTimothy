Quick Typography

Quick Typography is a Figma Plugin that makes it easy to edit typography in your designs. With a click, designers using Figma can change the font, font weight, size, & the line height without having to select them one after the other. Designed with Figma and developed with Javascript.

Why build Quick Typography?

I discovered that designers spend a lot of time selecting the text layers one after the other to change the font, font weight, size, & the line height. I wanted to make it easier for designers to change the typography in their designs with just a click on the frame. I also wanted to make sure that the designers are using the correct font for the project.
